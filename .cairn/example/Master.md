## What This Project Is

Sourdough Lab is a bi-weekly podcast about bread-making at the intermediate-to-advanced level. Each episode pairs one professional baker with one serious home baker around a single topic where they genuinely disagree, and the host moderates the resulting argument.

The audience is the home baker who already knows the basics, has made dozens of loaves, and is looking for the kind of depth that pure beginner content does not provide and pure professional content does not translate.

---

## Format

- **Two-guest argument structure.** One pro, one serious home baker, one topic per episode.
- **25 minutes per episode**, edited tight. Closer to deliberate than baggy.
- **Bi-weekly** release cadence.
- **No filler episodes.** Two-guest argument only. Off-weeks stay off-weeks.
- **Host moderates lightly.** Sets up the topic, asks clarifying questions, keeps the exchange productive, closes with what each guest is taking away.

---

## Locked Decisions

| ID | Decision | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| F1 | Two-guest argument format | The format that doesn't exist on the market; the arguments are the interesting part |
| F2 | Target: intermediate home bakers | Excludes pure beginners and working professionals — the underserved middle |
| F3 | 25-minute duration, edited tight | Benchmarks favored deliberate over baggy; argument format fits in 25 min |
| F4 | Bi-weekly cadence | Sustainable with two-guest bookings and one host |
| F5 | No filler episodes | Preserve the core promise; every dilution example we looked at underperformed |
